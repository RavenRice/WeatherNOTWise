Help & FAQ

How do I search for a location?

Go to the Search page and type a city, zip code, or campus name.

How do I save favorites?

Click the Save Favorite button after searching for a location.

How are outfit tips generated?

Suggestions are based on temperature, rain chance, and wind speed.

What weather service is used?

WeatherWise uses the Open-Meteo Forecast API.
